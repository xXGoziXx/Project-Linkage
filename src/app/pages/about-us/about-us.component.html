About Us

The idea of Linkage is about combining elements of Anime and Fashion with Photography and Videography. This was labelled as a project that will one day become well known among Irish anime fans as well as fashionable people in Ireland and abroad.


This concept drove a team in 2020 to create Project_Linkage. The goal of this brand is to make clothing that is unique, stylish and comfortable for people who like anime, fashion or any creative element about the brand. We are grateful to everyone who has supported us so far because we strive to continuously grow in terms of fashion sense and creativity with the brand.


The name Linkage was made by combining the Naruto titles Hokage, Raikage, Kazekage, Mizukage and Tsuchikage. All those titles have the word "Kage" in them so to link the titles into one Linkage was made. The pronunciations are either "Lin-kidge" or "Lin-Ka-ge".
